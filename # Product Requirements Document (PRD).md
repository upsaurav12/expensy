# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

Personal Expense Analyzer

## Overview

Personal Expense Analyzer helps users understand where their money goes by automatically categorizing expenses into Fixed, Variable, and Semi-Variable categories and providing actionable insights.

The product targets users searching for:

* fixed expenses
* variable expenses
* monthly expenses
* expense categories
* how to track expenses
* budgeting basics

Instead of reading definitions, users receive personalized analysis.

---

## Problem Statement

Many people struggle to:

* Understand their spending habits
* Distinguish fixed and variable expenses
* Identify unnecessary spending
* Determine how much they can save each month

Most available content explains concepts but does not analyze the user's actual financial situation.

## MVP Scope

### Input

User enters:

* Monthly income
* Housing/Rent
* Utilities
* Internet
* Phone
* Insurance
* Transportation
* Food
* Shopping
* Entertainment
* Subscriptions
* Other expenses

---

### Processing Logic

System categorizes expenses into:

#### Fixed Expenses

Examples:

* Rent
* Insurance
* Loan payments
* Internet

#### Variable Expenses

Examples:

* Dining out
* Shopping
* Entertainment
* Fuel

#### Semi-Variable Expenses

Examples:

* Electricity
* Water
* Phone bill

---

### Output

#### Spending Summary

Display:

* Total income
* Total expenses
* Remaining balance

---

#### Expense Breakdown

Display:

* Fixed expenses amount
* Variable expenses amount
* Semi-variable expenses amount

Display both:

* Dollar value
* Percentage of income

---

#### Savings Analysis

Calculate:

* Savings amount
* Savings rate %

Example:

Income: $3000
Expenses: $2400
Savings: $600
Savings Rate: 20%

---

#### Personalized Insights

Generate 3–5 recommendations.

Examples:

* Housing costs exceed 45% of income.
* Dining expenses are unusually high.
* Subscription spending may be reduced.

---

#### Financial Health Score

Score from 0–100 based on:

* Savings rate
* Fixed expense ratio
* Debt ratio
* Expense distribution

Display:

Poor
Fair
Good
Excellent
